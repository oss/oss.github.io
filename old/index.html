Open System Solutions
Open System Solutions is composed of student system programmers employed by the Office of Information Technology. These dedicated workers stand at the forefront of the Open Source initiative here at Rutgers University. This group's primary focus is to incorporate Open Source software projects into an enterprise-level computing environment for the Rutgers community.

Many proud OSS alum have moved on to notable employers such as Pinterest, Amazon, Red Hat, Yahoo!, Goldman Sachs, Microsoft, Google, and MongoDB to name a few.

Notable projects include:
  • Go: The Rutgers URL Shortener
  • Official Rutgers University Android and iOS apps
